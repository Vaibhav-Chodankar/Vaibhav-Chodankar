Final Year Project

"Automated Water Level Monitoring system" was my final year project it was IoT based. We used Esp32 micro-controller, Relay modules, Water pumps and Ultrasonic sensors. Our system can individually monitor and control 12 tanks at same time. We can also replace water pump to an electric Valve if water comes from pipes rather then well. Also we can see the tanks water level from Blynk app which has virtual buttons to control (ON/OFF) water pumps.

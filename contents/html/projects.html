Automated Water Level Monitoring system

❮ ❯

I'm excited to showcase one of my innovative projects: an Automated Water Level Monitoring System. This project utilizes an Ultrasonic sensor, Relay modules, Submersible pumps, and an ESP32 microcontroller, all controlled via the Blynk app. It demonstrates my skills in IoT, hardware integration, and remote monitoring.

  • Ultrasonic Sensor: Utilized an ultrasonic sensor to accurately measure water levels in real-time, ensuring precise monitoring of tank status.
  • ESP32 Microcontroller: Leveraged the ESP32 microcontroller for its robust processing power and built-in Wi-Fi capabilities, enabling seamless data transmission and remote control.
  • Relay Modules and Submersible Pumps: Integrated relay modules to control the operation of submersible pumps, automating the process of water filling and drainage based on the monitored levels.
  • Blynk App Control: Implemented the Blynk app for remote control and monitoring, allowing users to view real-time water levels and control the pumps from anywhere, enhancing convenience and efficiency.
